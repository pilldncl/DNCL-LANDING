## The Core Problem

### **DNCL's Current Approach: B2B Brochure**
- Designed for enterprise buyers who already know you
- Assumes long sales cycle
- Requires explanation and relationship building
- Focus: **Credibility through process**

### **Phone Guys' Approach: E-commerce First, B2B Second**
- Designed for browsers who discover you
- Shortens path to conversion
- Shows value immediately through products
- Focus: **Desire through products, trust through service**
